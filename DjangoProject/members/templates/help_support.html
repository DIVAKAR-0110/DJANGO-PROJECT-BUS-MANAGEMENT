FAQs

  • How do I register for a bus pass? Fill out the registration form and submit your details.
  • What is the validity of the bus pass? The bus pass is valid for 6 months from the date of registration.
  • How do I renew my bus pass? You can renew your bus pass through the 'Renew' section of the portal.
  • What should I do if I lose my bus pass? Report it through the 'Cancel' section and apply for a new one.
